2. Description of Service

LoadTester provides on-demand distributed load testing services generating simulated traffic against target URLs and web applications. The Service operates in two modes: (a) Quick Load Test (Simple Mode): HTTP-level traffic generation at up to 100,000 requests per second from up to four geographic regions; and (b) Scenario Test: browser-based simulation using Playwright, with up to 500 concurrent virtual browsers executing user-defined scenarios.

The Service is designed for performance testing, capacity planning, and quality assurance of systems the Customer owns or is authorized to test. LoadTester operates as a one-shot, stateless service: we do not store customer data beyond the session lifetime, do not require account creation, and do not maintain ongoing customer relationships beyond the transaction.
