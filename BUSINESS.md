# Business Plan — LoadTester

## Product

Distributed load testing from 4 continents simultaneously. Pay per test, not per month.

## Market

Load testing tools range from open source (k6, Locust) to expensive SaaS (BlazeMeter $500/mo). Gap: easy distributed testing at affordable per-test pricing.

## Differentiation

1. **4 continents by default** — Real-world traffic, not synthetic from one datacenter
2. **Pay per test** — No monthly subscription, pay only when you test
3. **USDC payment** — Crypto-native, no credit card needed
4. **Domain verification** — Anti-DDoS protection built-in
5. **Real-time results** — Watch metrics stream during the test

## Pricing

| Tier | RPS | Duration | Locations | Price |
|------|-----|----------|-----------|-------|
| Free | 100 | 1 min | 1 | $0 |
| Pro | 10,000 | 10 min | All 4 | $20/test USDC |
| Business | 100,000 | 60 min | All 4 | $100/test USDC |

## Revenue Model

- Per-test payments in USDC
- 95% to operator, 5% Opsalis settlement
- No refunds (prepaid model)

## Target Customers

1. **DevOps teams** running pre-deployment load tests
2. **SaaS companies** testing global performance
3. **E-commerce** preparing for sale events
4. **Agencies** performance-testing client apps
